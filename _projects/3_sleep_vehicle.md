**Funding Agency:** Hyundai Motors (현대자동차)

**Period:** 2023.9 ~ 2024.8

The purpose of this research is to develop sleep induction technology utilizing music-based emotional vibration in vehicles, based on scientific evidence that auditory and tactile stimulation can improve sleep quality. The music-based content and technology developed through this research will optimize the in-vehicle sleep environment, allowing users to experience effective and high-quality sleep even during long-distance travel or traffic congestion.
